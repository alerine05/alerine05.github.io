Product Info

Description

With 7 types of spices, the seasoning offers a blend of unique taste and warmth.

The seasoning has no preservatives and colorants, so you can eat your food healthy and safe. Marination is not needed, which makes it convenient and quick!

Step 1: First, wash the chicken nuggets and dry the water.

Step 2: Dip the chicken nuggets evenly with powder, both inside and outside the skin. If the flesh color can't be seen, take off the excess powder.

Step 3: The oil must be hot and the chicken must be fully covered. You can start frying the chicken afterward.

Step 4: After putting down the chicken nuggets, turn it on medium heat and fry it slowly until it turns golden brown.

The product is a blend of 7 spices containing coriander powder, salt, curry powder, cumin powder, clove, anise, cinnamon.
